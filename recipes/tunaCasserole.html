Chef John's Tuna Noodle Casserole


Description

Tuna noodle casserole is a classic comfort food that is easy to make.
It is a great meal for a family dinner or a special occasion.
The creamy sauce and the tender noodles make this dish a favorite among many people.


Ingredients

  • 8 ounces egg noodles
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery

Instructions

  1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Gradually stir in the milk and cook until thickened.
  5. Stir in the cheese until melted.
  6. Stir in the tuna, onion, and celery.
  7. Stir in the cooked egg noodles.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
