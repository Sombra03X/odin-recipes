Chicken Carbonara


Description

Chicken carbonara pasta is a delicious pasta dish that is easy to make.
It is a great meal for a family dinner or a special occasion.
The creamy sauce and the tender chicken make this dish a favorite among many people.


Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan.
  3. Cook the bacon in a large skillet until crispy, then remove and chop.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through.
  5. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Stir in the cream and bring to a simmer.
  7. Stir in the egg mixture and cook until thickened.
  8. Stir in the peas, parsley, and bacon.
  9. Stir in the cooked spaghetti and toss to coat.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
