Chilly dog Mac and Cheese


Description

Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food that is easy to make.
It is a great meal for a family dinner or a special occasion.
The creamy cheese sauce and the tender pasta make this dish a favorite among many people.


Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 hot dogs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped jalapeno pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the flour, salt, pepper, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Gradually stir in the milk and cook until thickened.
  5. Stir in the cheese until melted.
  6. Stir in the macaroni and hot dogs.
  7. Stir in the onion, bell peppers, jalapeno pepper, tomato, green onion, and parsley.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
